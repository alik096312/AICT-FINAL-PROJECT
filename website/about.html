Meet the history of our company

1850

"Our journey began in a quiet village where our founder baked the very first batch of cookies. Using family recipes passed down for generations, every cookie was made with care and love. These homemade treats quickly became a favorite among the locals, bringing people together over tea and laughter. This humble beginning planted the seeds for our cookie legacy."

1897

"In this year, we introduced our iconic chocolate chip cookies, which became an instant hit. The bakery grew busier as people came from nearby towns to taste these sweet delights. It was also a time when we started experimenting with new flavors and ingredients, adding more variety to our menu.. This marked the beginning of our commitment to innovation in baking."

>

1951

"Our bakery took a big step forward by opening its first large kitchen to meet the growing demand. This year, we also launched our famous butter cookies in tin boxes, which quickly became a popular gift choice. Families loved sharing these cookies during holidays and special occasions. The expansion allowed us to reach more people and spread happiness through our baking."

2011

"Stepping into the modern world, we introduced online ordering to make our cookies more accessible. Now, customers could enjoy fresh cookies delivered right to their homes. We also expanded our range to include gluten-free and vegan options, ensuring everyone could enjoy our treats. This year marked a new chapter of growth and connection with our customers worldwide."
